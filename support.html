Frequently Asked Questions

Is Aurevia free to use?
Yes! Aurevia is completely free with no in-app purchases or subscriptions required.
Is my data safe?
Absolutely. All your data is stored locally on your device only. We don't transmit any personal information to external servers. Your privacy is our top priority.
Do I need to create an account?
No account is required! Simply download the app and start learning. All your progress is saved locally on your device.
What iOS version do I need?
Aurevia requires iOS 17.0 or later. Make sure your device is updated to the latest iOS version for the best experience.
How do I reset my progress?
Since all data is stored locally, you can reset your progress by deleting and reinstalling the app. Note that this will permanently delete all your saved data.
Can I use Aurevia offline?
Yes! Most features work completely offline. The only exception is accessing external scholarship websites, which requires an internet connection.
Are the scholarships legitimate?
Yes. We only link to reputable scholarship providers like Fastweb, Scholarships.com, and College Board. Always verify scholarship details on their official websites.
I found a bug. How do I report it?
Please email us at aurevia.app@gmail.com with details about the issue, including your device model and iOS version. We'll work to fix it as soon as possible!
Can I suggest new features?
We'd love to hear your ideas! Send your suggestions to aurevia.app@gmail.com. We're constantly working to improve Aurevia based on user feedback.

Technical Support

If you're experiencing technical issues with Aurevia, please try these steps first:

  • Restart the app
  • Make sure you're running the latest version of Aurevia from the App Store
  • Check that your device is running iOS 17.0 or later
  • Restart your device
  • If problems persist, contact us at aurevia.app@gmail.com
