Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2, 2026

Introduction

Aurevia ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Aurevia mobile application (the "App").

Quick Summary: Your data stays on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or share any personal information with external servers.

Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Aurevia stores the following data locally on your device only using iOS UserDefaults:

  • User Profile Information
    • Display name (e.g., "Aurevia User")
    • User ID (locally generated)
    • Email address (if provided)
  • App Usage Data
    • Quiz progress and scores
    • Budget entries and categories
    • Game progress and achievements
    • Scholarship bookmarks and filters
    • Dashboard metrics and activity logs
    • Navigation history within the app
  • Preferences and Settings
    • App theme preferences
    • Feature usage metrics

Information We DO NOT Collect

  • ✗ We do NOT transmit any personal data to external servers
  • ✗ We do NOT track your location
  • ✗ We do NOT access your contacts, photos, or other device data
  • ✗ We do NOT use analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase)
  • ✗ We do NOT collect biometric data
  • ✗ We do NOT use advertising identifiers or tracking pixels

How We Use Your Information

All data collected is used solely to:

  • Provide core app functionality (quizzes, budgets, games, scholarships)
  • Save your progress and preferences
  • Display personalized content within the app
  • Track your learning journey and financial literacy progress
✓ Your data never leaves your device.

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure UserDefaults system
  • Data is encrypted by iOS's built-in encryption when your device is locked
  • No cloud storage or external servers are used
  • If you delete the app, all your data is permanently removed from your device

Third-Party Services

External Links

The App contains links to external scholarship websites and educational resources. When you click these links:

  • You will be directed to third-party websites
  • Those websites have their own privacy policies
  • We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites
  • We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any websites you visit

Scholarship websites we link to may include:

  • fastweb.com
  • scholarships.com
  • collegeboard.org
  • And other legitimate scholarship providers

We do not send any of your personal information to these websites unless you voluntarily provide it directly on their platforms.

Children's Privacy

Aurevia is designed for students and young adults, including users under 13 years of age. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do not collect any personal information from users
  • We do not require account creation or login credentials
  • All data remains on the user's device
  • Parents can delete all app data by deleting the app from the device

Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: All data is visible within the app
  • Delete your data: Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all local data
  • Export your data: Currently not supported, but data is not stored externally
  • Opt-out of tracking: No tracking occurs, so no opt-out is needed

Data Retention

  • Data is retained on your device until you delete the app
  • No backup copies are stored on our servers
  • No data is retained after app deletion

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy within the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

Your continued use of the App after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

International Data Transfers

Since all data is stored locally on your device, there are no international data transfers.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect. As stated in this policy:

  • We do not sell your personal information
  • We do not share your personal information with third parties
  • All data is stored locally on your device

European Union Users (GDPR)

For users in the European Union:

  • Legal Basis: We process data based on your consent and to fulfill the app's core functionality
  • Data Controller: Aurevia is the data controller
  • Right to Erasure: Delete the app to remove all data
  • Data Portability: Not applicable (no server-side storage)
